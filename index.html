About IIUCCPS Ladder

This ladder is a dynamic practice tool designed to help competitive programmers at IIUC (and the broader community) improve their problem-solving skills in a structured way.

  • Curated Problem List: The Problems of the Ladder are handpicked by some experienced programmers of IIUCCPS.
  • Live Status Sync: Enter your Codeforces handle to see which problems you've already solved, updated in real-time.
  • Dynamic Filtering: Filter problems by rating and tags to target specific areas for improvement.
  • Clean & Modern UI: A distraction-free, themeable interface to help you focus on what matters: solving problems.
